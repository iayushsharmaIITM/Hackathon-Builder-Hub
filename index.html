What is Claude Builder Club?

Claude Builder Clubs are Anthropic's official student-led campus communities. Backed by the Claude for Education initiative, club leads collaborate with Anthropic's research, product, and education teams to set up organizations for AI builders at universities globally — running technical workshops, hackathons, and demo nights to help students explore AI's potential responsibly.

Backed by Anthropic

Collaborate directly with Anthropic's research, product, and education teams. Club leads receive a paid stipend and direct access to frontier AI development.

Your Feedback Shapes Claude

Student needs aren't hypothetical to Anthropic. Your input and feedback from campus events directly influences Claude's development and educational features.

Find Your People

Connect with students who see AI as a tool for expanding human capability, not replacing it. Build a network of builders, creators, and future AI leaders.

What we do

Regular

Workshops & Guest Speakers

Organize technical and non-technical workshops featuring Anthropic guest speakers. Deep-dive into prompt engineering, Claude Code, and building agents.

Flagship

Hackathons & Demo Nights

Run campus hackathons and demo nights for student builders. Compete, ship real projects with Claude, and showcase winning builds to the community.

Ongoing

API Credits & Resources

Share API credits, tips, starter kits, and resources with club members. Get hands-on access to Claude's frontier models and tools like Claude Code.

Impact

Shape the Future of AI

Directly influence Claude's development through student feedback. Help advance AI education and promote responsible, safe use of AI on your campus.
