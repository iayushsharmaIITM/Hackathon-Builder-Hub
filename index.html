What is Claude Builder Club?

We're the official Claude Builder community at IIT Madras. A space where students and creators come together to learn, build, and ship AI-powered products using Anthropic's Claude platform.

Community First

Connect with fellow IIT Madras builders. Collaborate on projects, exchange ideas, and grow your network.

Cutting-Edge Tools

Access Claude APIs, prompt libraries, starter kits, and exclusive tooling that accelerates your workflow.

Learn, Build & Ship

Weekly sessions, hackathons, and mentorship — go from idea to deployed product in record time.

What we do

Weekly

Build Sessions

Live co-working sessions. Build in public, share progress, get feedback from peers and mentors.

Monthly

Hackathons

Themed hackathons with prizes, mentorship, and visibility. Build fast, win big, get noticed.

Biweekly

Expert Office Hours

1-on-1 sessions with AI engineers, product leaders, and founders who've scaled AI products.

Always Open

Curated prompts, open-source templates for chatbots, agents, RAG pipelines. Ship in hours.
